Services

Through a RESTful Application Programming Interface (API), any client may interface with the KeyTerms to access and manage your Component's vetted terms or personal terms.

Search Terms

Any organization may create lists of "terms" (specialized subject matter including formulaic phraseology, organizations, etc that do not typically appear in dictionaries).

Share Terms

Shareable lists allow individuals organizations to maintain authoritative control over their own terminology and easily import and export terms from other organizations that share their lists.

Nominate Terms

Improve translation consistency in an organization by establishing a process of nominations of terms from personal use up to the Component's use.

Manage User Terms

Not ready to share your term with your organization? Keep your individual terms in the KeyTerms and improve it until you're ready.

Tag Terms

Organized by domains, the terms are domain tagged by the same users who use them
